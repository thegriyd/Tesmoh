We advise you, you call the right decision!

Tesmoh International Ltd aims at bridging the common gaps that exist between companies in one region and the opportunities for trade and investment that exist in another. We understand the finer details of trade missions, how your business can take full advantage of them, and which partner and investment businesses would be the ideal fit for you. Another potent aspect of global trade and investing is being aware of what opportunities exist throughout your own region. Most business owners often fail to recognize the potential that exists right there in their own backyard. By partnering with other companies from overseas to expand within your region, incredible opportunities exist to continue domestic growth as well. Our experts understand this very well and are committed to helping your company achieve this.

Our Story

We understand that the target region’s socio-political-economic, taxation and investment policies plays a key role in attracting investments. Our in-house economists, market research partners and senior on-ground personnel in different countries continuously produce and update research on macro aspects, as well as specific research on the ICT, Agriculture, Renewable Energy, Advanced Manufacturing, Biotech and Food & Beverages sectors within these countries.
We complement this view of the markets with research on investment and expansion trends in the global corporate sector (in these different industries). One of the key reasons for our efficient and tive Lead Generation solutions is that a high portion of our time and resources is spent on primary, secondary and third-party independent research, in order to develop a deep, firm-wide understanding of the markets that we cater to. Based on our understanding of the target industries across multiple countries, we chalk out a customized strategy with the clients to achieve the desired results. Based on the target industries in the investment destination, we incorporate different elements of lead generation such as setting up a local office and website for the client, designing and running marketing promotions, setting up meetings with our existing “Family of Clients”, attending trade and industry conferences, and setting up dedicated and experienced personnel to handle different lead generation channels. The activities are supported by the latest client reporting and project management tools.
